Ado Evergreen Dairy Farm

Sebeta, Oromia | Milk, Cheese, Yogurt Production

Executive Summary

Ado Evergreen Dairy Farm is a student-founded dairy production farm that provides fresh milk, cheese, and yogurt. Located in Sebeta, Oromia, the business is committed to producing high-quality dairy products through sustainable practices and innovation.

Company Description

The farm was established by 8 students with a passion for agriculture and sustainability. Starting with 25 dairy cows, the business focuses on ethical practices, youth employment, and boosting the dairy sector in Ethiopia.

Market Analysis

Our target market includes local households, supermarkets, restaurants, and institutions. We offer premium, fresh, locally produced dairy products that stand out in both quality and transparency.

Production Plan

We began with 25 high-yielding cows (Holstein, Jersey, Boran, Arsi) and plan to double our herd in two years. Focus areas: milk quality, hygiene, and animal welfare.
